Single Responsibility BEM: 2nd Pen

This is a fork of Single Responsibility BEM applied in a practical sense. It's a more polished example than the original pen, which was more like a quick and dirty exploration of its concepts. I'll be writing up a post to explain everything that's going on over the weekend. I'm hoping it will be a boon to solo developers and teams, or at least spark conversation.

Pros

  • Encourages building out from components.
  • Keeps modules in their own scope to be moved around.
  • Doesn't make you to repeat the Block.
  • A single, extendable .augment class to build out modifiers.

Cons

  • Pretty much requires Sass.
  • Adjoining classes aren't supported in IE6.
  • May require a shift in thought.
