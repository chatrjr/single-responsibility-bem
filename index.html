Single Responsibility BEM: 2nd Pen

This is a fork of Single Responsibility BEM applied in a practical sense. It's a more polished example than the original pen, which was more like a quick and dirty exploration of its concepts. I'll be writing up a post to explain in detail. I'm hoping it will spark conversation. The gist of this approach is the value in designing from components and embracing systems for more cohesive, maintainable results.

Single Responsibility BEM is a Different Approach to BEM

Pros

  • Encourages building out from components.
  • Keeps modules in their own scope to be moved around.
  • A single, extendable .augment “namespace” class to build out modifiers.

Cons

  • Pretty much requires Sass.
  • Adjoining classes aren't supported in IE6.
  • May require a shift in thought.
